Hey There!

I am Siya Choudhary a high schooler, self-taught artist, and tech enthusiast. Art found its way into me before I could barely form sentences. Every-time I lifted a crayon or pencil my mind wandered as I drew numerous drawings each with a story behind it. As I grew I began exploring the world of art even deeper and soon started working with various mediums. The creativity that I gained from practicing art led me to web development and for the last few years I have been creating web apps and numerous online games. I am also an AP Computer Science student striving to challenge myself and learn more everyday. My goal is to pursue art and utilize my creativity to make the world a more colorful and happier place.

Art Commissions

I have been working with Anupama Dalmia, an author, blogger, and public figure based in India, since 2018. My work has been featured on multiple different topics for both Anupama Dalmia's personal and Public work including her platform 'Beyond the box'.
